Bio10-Kane - Advanced Small Insect Pest Defence


General Description

In modern agriculture, protecting crops from spider-mites poses a significant challenge, especially when employing organic methods. While traditional chemical pesticides can be effective, they carry considerable risks to the environment and to health, fuelling the demand for natural and eco-friendly alternatives. In response to this need, we developed Bio10-Kane, a specialised pest insect defence system. Our solution not only shields crops from small insect pests such as thrips and spider-mites, but also nourishes plants to enhance their natural defences. By combining sea-mineral-based compounds with finely micronised diatomaceous earth (DE) and a natural biopolymer, Bio10-Kane offers a robust, environmentally friendly alternative that tackles the difficulties of organic spider-mite control.

Current Update

As of mid-2024, Bio10-Kane has proven to be a groundbreaking solution for managing small insect pests, including thrips and spider- mites. Our advanced pest defence system has achieved notable successes and advancements, including:

  • Effective Performance: Bio10-Kane has demonstrated significant efficacy in targeting small insect pests. The combination of sea-mineral-based compounds, finely micronized diatomaceous earth (DE), and a natural biopolymer has been validated through extensive application trials, proving its ability to protect crops.
  • Enhanced Formulation: We have optimized the product’s formulation, improving the synergy between sea minerals, micronized DE, and the natural biopolymer. This refinement has enhanced the product’s protective barrier and targeted pest control capabilities.
  • Successful Initial Deployments: Early adoption by farmers and greenhouse operators has confirmed Bio10-Kane’s ability to maintain healthy, pest-free plants and support sustainable agricultural practices.

What's Next
