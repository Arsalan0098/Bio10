BIO10-GREEN - NATURAL FERTILISING SOLUTIONS

Revolutionizing Plant Nutrition with Enzyme-Digested Sprouted Grains


General Description

The need for natural and effective fertilizers has become increasingly significant in sustainable agriculture. Bio10-Green addresses this need with an advanced plant-based organic liquid fertilizer that leverages innovative processes to enhance plant nutrition. This product combines enzyme-digested sprouted grains with volcanic rock phosphate to deliver a highly bioavailable and nutrient-rich solution. The utilization of enzyme-digestion ensures that nutrients are more readily accessible to plants, while volcanic rock phosphate provides a steady release of essential phosphorus. This unique formulation aims to improve plant health and growth while supporting sustainable farming practices.


Research History:

Phase 1: Initial Concept and Feasibility Study

  • Year: 2020
  • Description: The project began with exploring the potential of enzyme-digestion in sprouted grains as a basis for an organic liquid fertilizer. Initial research focused on the effectiveness in improving nutrient availability.

Phase 2: Laboratory Testing and Optimization

  • Year: 2021
  • Description: Extensive tests were conducted to optimize the formulation, including blending ratios and enzyme activity levels. Various organic materials were evaluated to ensure the product's effectiveness.

Phase 3: Prototype Development and Initial Trials

  • Year: 2021
  • Description: A prototype of the liquid fertilizer was developed and tested in controlled environments. Initial trials assessed nutrient release, plant response, and formulation stability.

Phase 4: Field Trials and Refinement

  • Year: 2022
  • Description: Field trials were conducted to test the product in real-world agricultural settings. Feedback from these trials informed adjustments to the formulation and application methods.

Phase 5: Scaling and Commercialization

  • Year: 2022 - Current
  • Description: Following successful field trials, the product is being scaled up for commercial production. Collaboration with agricultural experts continues to refine the product for market readiness.

Phase 6: Ongoing Research and Improvement

  • Year: Current
  • Description: Continuous research focuses on optimizing the fertilizer's formulation and exploring new applications. Customer feedback and advancements in science drive ongoing enhancements.

Current Update

As of mid-2024, Bio10-Green has made considerable progress in its development and application:

  • Formulation Success: The combination of enzyme-digested sprouted grains and volcanic rock phosphate has been successfully formulated into a highly effective liquid fertilizer. Early results indicate a significant improvement in nutrient uptake and plant growth compared to traditional fertilizers.
  • Positive Trial Outcomes: Initial field trials have demonstrated that Bio10-Green enhances plant health, yields, and overall soil fertility. Feedback from these trials has highlighted the product's ease of use and effectiveness in various agricultural environments, including urban gardens and greenhouses.
  • Product Refinement: Based on trial feedback, adjustments to the formulation have been made to optimize nutrient delivery and ensure stability in the liquid form. These refinements have improved the consistency and performance of the fertilizer.

What's Next

Looking ahead, we are focusing on several key areas to further enhance Bio10- Green and to expand its impact:

  • Extended Field Trials: We plan to conduct additional field trials across a broader range of agricultural settings to validate the product's performance under different conditions and with various crop types. These trials will help to further refine the product and confirm its benefits.
  • Product Line Development: We are developing complementary products to address specific nutrient needs and application methods. This could include formulations for specialized crops or different growth stages.
  • Ongoing R&D: Continuous research will focus on enhancing the formulation and exploring new scientific advancements to improve the product further. We are committed to incorporating user feedback and staying at the forefront of sustainable agriculture technology.
