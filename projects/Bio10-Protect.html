Bio10-Protect - Advanced Pest Insect defence


General Description

In modern agriculture, the need for sustainable and effective pest management solutions has never been greater. Traditional chemical pesticides, while effective, pose significant environmental and health risks, leading to a growing demand for natural and eco-friendly alternatives.

To address this burgeoning problem, we developed Bio10-Protect, a dual-action pest insect defence system. Our goal was to create a solution that not only shields crops from a wide range of insect pests but also nourishes plants, enhancing their natural defence mechanisms. Bio10-Protect combines plant-based fertilising compounds with suspended diatomaceous earth (DE) and a natural nano biopolymer, offering a robust, environmentally friendly alternative to conventional pest control methods.


Research History:

Phase 1: Initial Concept and Feasibility Study

  • Year:2020
  • Description: Initial studies explored the potential of combining plant-based nutrients with DE for dual-action plant health and pest protection.

Phase 2: Laboratory Testing and Optimization

  • Year:2022
  • Description:Extensive laboratory tests were conducted to optimise the formulation, ensuring effective nutrient delivery and pest protection.

Phase 3: Prototype Development and Field Trials

  • Year:2023
  • Description:Prototypes were developed and subjected to field trials to assess performance in various agricultural settings. Nano biopolymer was incorporated into the formulation.

Phase 4: Scaling and Commercialisation

  • Year:2023 - Current
  • Description:Successful field trials led to the scaling up of production for commercial use, with input from agricultural experts.

Phase 5: Ongoing Research and Improvement

  • Year:Current
  • Description:Continuous research and development efforts are underway to enhance the formulation and application methods.

Current Update

As of mid-2024, Bio10-Protect has made significant strides in advancing pest management solutions. Following its successful rollout, the product has demonstrated positive performance in diverse agricultural environments, effectively shielding crops from a wide range of insect pests while enhancing plant health. Key accomplishments include:

  • Successful Prototype Testing: Initial prototype testing has confirmed the effectiveness of Bio10-Protect in protecting against insect pests and promoting plant growth. Early feedback has been overwhelmingly positive, highlighting the product’s dual-action benefits.
  • Optimised Formulation:Based on prototype data, we have fine-tuned the formulation to maximise the synergy between plant-based nutrients, diatomaceous earth (DE), and the natural nano biopolymer. This optimisation has enhanced the product's efficacy in creating a robust barrier against pests.
  • Increased Production Capacity:We have scaled up production systems to meet growing demand, ensuring that Bio10-Protect is readily available for agricultural use. Our manufacturing processes have been refined to maintain high quality and consistency.

What's Next

ALooking ahead, we are focusing on several key areas to further enhance Bio10-Protect and expand its impact:

  • Broader Application Testing: We plan to conduct additional field trials in new agricultural settings and with different crop types to further validate the product's versatility and effectiveness. This includes testing in various climatic conditions and soil types to ensure optimal performance across diverse environments.
  • Innovation in Application Methods:Research is underway to explore new application methods that could enhance the efficiency and ease of using Bio10-Protect. This may involve developing more user-friendly application technologies or exploring different delivery systems.
  • We are considering the development of complementary products that build on the success of Bio10- Protect. This could integrate additional natural ingredients for broader pest management.
  • Ongoing Research and Development:Continuous R&D efforts will focus on improving the formulation based on real-world feedback and new scientific discoveries. We are committed to staying at the forefront of sustainable pest management technologies.
