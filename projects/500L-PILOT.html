500L PILOT BIOPROCESS REACTOR

Advancing Bioprocessing with Scalable Pilot Reactors

CONTACT US

General Description

The 500L Pilot Bioprocess Reactor is an ongoing project that aims to advance scalable bioprocessing technology, facilitating the transition from lab-scale research to commercial applications. The project was initiated with the goal of producing a consistent supply of microalgal protein for use in agrochemicals as a nitrogen source. During this journey, we realized that other biotechnology startups and researchers faced the same issue we did—a lack of access to pilot reactors for scaling their ideas to a commercial level. Now, alongside our internal developments, we are focused on providing this technology to support others in the industry who are facing similar challenges.

This reactor is being developed to offer flexibility for a wide range of biotechnological processes, including precision fermentation and enzyme production. It provides an efficient and controlled environment that is crucial for scaling up while maintaining precise control over critical parameters. This project addresses the growing need for practical, scalable solutions that bridge the gap between research and industrial-scale production, supporting the biotech industry's evolving demands.


Project Development

The development of the 500L pilot reactor is an active, collaborative effort. It started with designing a system capable of optimizing key bioprocess parameters such as temperature, aeration, and pH. The reactor is being developed with advanced control systems that allow real-time monitoring and adjustments, ensuring that microbial growth and product yields are optimized.

Ongoing trials are being conducted to fine-tune the system's performance across a variety of bioprocesses. The design has been iterated several times to ensure the reactor can handle the complexity of different biological systems, from enzyme production to microbial fermentation. The chosen 500L capacity provides a practical scale-up pathway, allowing researchers and industry experts to validate and optimize processes before moving to full-scale production.

Key Features

  • Scalable Design: The 500L capacity bridges the gap between small lab-scale reactors and full-scale industrial systems, facilitating seamless scale-up of bioprocesses.
  • Real-Time Monitoring & Control: Our reactor is equipped with state-of-the-art sensors and control systems, allowing precise management of critical variables like temperature, pH, and aeration.
  • Flexible Applications: The reactor supports a wide range of bioprocesses, from microbial fermentation to enzyme production, making it adaptable for diverse industries.
  • Ongoing Validation: Active trials are being conducted to validate the reactor’s performance and ensure it delivers reliable results, enabling a smoother transition from research to production.
  • Sustainable Bioprocessing: Designed with sustainability in mind, the reactor minimizes energy consumption and waste, aligning with the growing demand for eco-friendly industrial practices.

    • Potential Impact

    As the project progresses, the 500L Pilot Bioprocess Reactor is expected to have a transformative effect on industries such as pharmaceuticals, agriculture, and food production. The ability to precisely scale bioprocesses from the lab to industrial applications will reduce the risks and costs associated with full- scale production.

    By offering a pilot-scale solution, this reactor helps researchers and companies explore innovative solutions like precision fermentation and enzyme production, providing a pathway to turn experimental research into viable, market-ready products.


    Next Steps

    The next phase of this ongoing project will focus on expanding the reactor’s application scope, refining control systems, and ensuring process efficiency. As development progresses, the reactor is expected to play a key role in enabling scalable and sustainable bioprocessing for a wide range of industries.
